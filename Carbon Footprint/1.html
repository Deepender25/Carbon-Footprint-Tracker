Welcome to the Carbon Footprint Tracker!

-This application helps you monitor and reduce your carbon emissions through daily activity tracking.

-Our planet's health is more important than ever, and understanding your carbon footprint is a crucial step in reducing your impact on the environment. The Carbon Footprint Tracker is here to help you easily measure and monitor the emissions you create through daily activities like transportation, energy use, and consumption habits. Together, we can work toward a more sustainable future. Start tracking today and take the first step toward a greener lifestyle!

-Our calculator provides a simple way to estimate your daily carbon footprint based on your transportation, electricity use, and food consumption. By inputting your daily activities, you can understand your impact on the environment and receive personalized suggestions for reduction.

Solutions and Impact on Daily Life

The Carbon Footprint Tracker offers several solutions that can positively impact our daily lives:

1. Enhanced Awareness

By quantifying carbon emissions from everyday activities, users become more aware of their environmental impact, prompting more sustainable choices.

2. Personalized Recommendations

The app provides tailored suggestions to reduce carbon footprints, such as alternative transportation methods, energy-saving tips, and dietary changes.

3. Community Engagement

Users can participate in eco-friendly challenges, fostering a sense of community and shared responsibility for sustainability.

4. Data-Driven Decisions

With access to their own data, users can track progress over time and make informed decisions about their lifestyles, contributing to a lower overall carbon footprint.

5. Supporting Global Goals

This project aligns with global sustainability goals, encouraging individuals to contribute to larger efforts against climate change.

Formulas for Carbon Footprint Calculation

To calculate your carbon footprint, we use the following formulas:

  • Transportation Footprint:
    Carbon Emissions (kg CO2) = Kilometers Driven 0.125 kg CO2/Km
  • Electricity Footprint:
    Carbon Emissions (kg CO2) = kWh Used 0.82 kg CO2/kWh
  • Disel Footprint:
    Carbon Emissions (kg CO2) = kWh Used 0.82 kg CO2/kWh
  • Petrol Footprint:
    Carbon Emissions (kg CO2) = kWh Used 0.82 kg CO2/kWh
  • CNG Footprint:
    Carbon Emissions (kg CO2) = kWh Used 0.82 kg CO2/kWh
  • Veg Food Footprint:
    Carbon Emissions (kg CO2) = Meals Eaten 0.723 kg CO2/meal
  • Non-Veg Food Footprint:
    Carbon Emissions (kg CO2) = Meals Eaten 4.8 kg CO2/meal

Total Carbon Footprint:
Total Carbon Emissions (kg CO2) = Transportation Footprint + Electricity Footprint + Food Footprint
